Sitaphal Ice Cream

When it comes to ice cream, few things can beat the delight of savouring a flavour that’s both unique and perfect. Naturals Ice Cream, known for its commitment to quality and innovation, brings you an exquisite seasonal treat: Sitaphal Ice Cream.

The charm of Sitaphal

Sitaphal is a beloved fruit in India, often enjoyed for its luscious taste. Rich in antioxidants, this fruit is simply perfect. Besides, the texture of Sitaphal is also like the fruit, unique in every bite – its creamy flesh is interspersed with seeds, which makes it a bit of a challenge to eat directly. However, Naturals Ice Cream has ingeniously transformed it into a smooth, delectable ice cream, making it both – easier and enjoyable to consume. Ice Cream is renowned for its dedication to using fresh, natural ingredients. Unlike many other brands that rely on artificial flavours and preservatives, Naturals focuses on authenticity. The Sitaphal Ice Cream is no exception. Each batch is made with real custard apples, ensuring that you get the genuine taste of the fruit in every bite. The result is an ice cream that is not only delicious but also free from artificial additives, making it a great choice for ice cream lovers.

Seasonal delight

Custard apples are typically harvested between September and December, which means this ice cream is available for a limited time each year. This seasonal nature adds to the allure, making it a must-try treat during its availability. The anticipation of waiting for this flavour makes it even more special, much like waiting for mangoes in the summer.
